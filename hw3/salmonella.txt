CONCLUSIONS

It's overwhelmingly clear that this is Salmonella enterica DNA. All of the best
matches are of serovar Typhimurium, but several others (Schwarzengrund,
Paratyphi, Heidelberg) also match closely, signifying that although they have
slightly different DNA all of these serotypes produce some similar proteins.
Several different species of bacteria (E. coli, P. alcalifaciens) produce
similarly-functioning proteins with sections similar to parts of ORF 1,
implying that some of these proteins exhibit not only cross-serotype but also
cross-genus similarities.
